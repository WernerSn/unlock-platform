Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Billing

How do I change my subscription plan?

You can change your subscription plan by going to Settings > Subscription. Choose your new plan and follow the prompts to complete the change.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal. For enterprise customers, we also support wire transfers.

Features & Usage

How do I create custom reports?

To create a custom report, navigate to the Reports section and click "New Report". You can then choose from various templates or start from scratch.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your data in various formats including CSV, Excel, and PDF. Look for the export button in the top right of any report view.
